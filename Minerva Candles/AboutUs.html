Our Story...


Ours is a success story that will make your heart warm. A young girl had a dream of spreading happiness and becoming independent and able to support her old parents. She thought of ways to intwine the two and found the perfect harmoney that worked for her, combining her love for scented candles with her ambition.


Starting off as a one woman team in the back of a garage and a university fragrance lab, we now have our own warehouse that houses our own manufacturing unit and fragrance lab that meets international standards. Our candles are still made with the utmost love and care, we've only gotten better with time as is reflected in our growing sales over time.


All our candles are vegan along with a minimum carbon footprint, making them your first eco-friendly scented candle choice. Our ingredients are sourced from all over the world and are 100% natural.


When you buy from Minerva, you won't regret it..
